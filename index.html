about us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, GIN Bakers began with a vision to create baked goods that bring joy and comfort. Starting with family recipes, we've grown from a small kitchen to a beloved bakery known for quality and flavor in every bite. Our treats are crafted to make life's moments sweeter and more memorable.

Our Ingredients

We believe that exceptional taste begins with exceptional ingredients. That's why we carefully select the finest local and organic ingredients, from creamy butter to fresh fruits, ensuring that every bite is packed with genuine flavor. With no artificial additives, our treats are as wholesome as they are delicious.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to bring joy to your everyday life with treats that are crafted with care and passion. We're honored to be a part of your celebrations, small or big, by delivering baked goods that make each occasion special. At GIN Bakers, every bite is a little taste of happiness.

FAQ

How does it work?

Our bakery offers a thoughtfully curated menu featuring unique, high-quality baked goods like cookies, waffles, and pastries.

How long does it take for delivery?

Local Deliveries:
Orders are typically delivered within 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the customer's location and the complexity of the order.
Customized Orders:
For special or bulk orders (e.g., personalized cakes or large batches of baked goods), delivery may take 2 to 4 hours or as communicated during order confirmation.
Peak Times:
During busy hours or festive seasons, delivery times might extend slightly, but we always strive to keep customers informed about any delays.

Can I order for huge parties?

Yes, we accepts large orders for parties. Place your order 2-3 days in advance to ensure freshness and timely preparation. Customizations, bulk discounts, and delivery services are available. Contact us to plan your order! üéâüç∞

How much protein it contains?

Cookies: Around 2-4g of protein per serving (depending on ingredients like nuts or chocolate).
Waffles: Typically 4-6g of protein per waffle.
Pastries: Generally 3-5g of protein per serving, depending on the filling and recipe.

Is it cooked with oil?

Most of our baked goods, such as cookies, pastries, and waffles, are baked and not cooked with oil. However, some recipes may include small amounts of butter, oil, or other fats to enhance flavor and texture. For detailed ingredient information, please check the product recipe or contact us directly.
